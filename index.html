Services We Provide

Property Marketing

Effective rental property marketing is essential for securing reliable tenants quickly.
We employ a blend of proven marketing techniques to maximize your rental income. Our strategy includes:

  • Online Marketing: We leverage local platforms, social media, and compelling property descriptions to attract potential tenants.
  • Offline Marketing: With our extensive network of real estate brokers and landlord referrals, we ensure your property reaches a wide audience.
  • Community Connections: Our strong relationships with NGOs and the diplomatic community further enhance our marketing efforts, helping us find the right tenants for your property.

Rent Collection

We prioritize timely and secure rent collection in accordance with local laws and lease agreements. Our process includes:

  • Direct Deposits: Rent is deposited directly into your bank account for convenience.
  • Verification: Both tenants and property owners confirm the deposit, ensuring transparency.
  • Proactive Measures: Should issues arise, we take additional steps to collect rent or initiate eviction processes when necessary.

Maintenance

Proper maintenance is vital for preserving your property’s value and ensuring tenant satisfaction. Our comprehensive maintenance services include:

  • Routine Upkeep and Inspection: We handle day-to-day maintenance tasks to keep your property in excellent condition and arrange routine inspections.
  • Emergency Repairs: Our team is always ready to respond swiftly to urgent repair requests.
  • Move-In Rehab: We prepare properties for new tenants, ensuring they are in top shape upon move-in.

About Us

Emnet Property Management was founded in 2024 by Nebiyou Elias Meshesha in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

With over 17 years of experience in real estate and property management in Texas, USA, Nebiyou has established a reputation for excellence. He has worked with renowned U.S. brokerages such as Keller Williams Realty, REMAX, and EXP Realty, and has successfully managed over 169 properties through his own brokerages, DFW Horns Property Management LLC and The NEBSTER Realty LLC.

Nebiyou's educational background includes a B.S. in International Business Marketing from San Francisco State University. He has also served as a business analyst and consultant for major U.S. consulting firms, specializing in ERP and CRM software implementations.

Fluent in English and French, Nebiyou brings a global perspective to his work. After relocating to Ethiopia in 2022, Nebiyou identified a need for a property management company that prioritizes exceptional service, cutting-edge technology, and transparency. As a seasoned real estate investor in both the U.S. and Ethiopia, he understands the complexities of property ownership and is committed to delivering unparalleled service to clients.
